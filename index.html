Impacts

Since inception, ESF has helped place hundreds of needy school leavers in different colleges and high schools. This has increased transition from primary schools to secondary schools, and also from secondary schools to colleges

What We Do

We sensitize the public and encourage needy students including OVCs to access secondary and tertiary education as we provide local support- from the point a potential student makes an inquiry up until the point when they complete their studies

By joining colleges, students can enhance their skills, knowledge, and career progression as they study with the reputable institutions in Kenya.Through our collaboration with various colleges, students can access a wide range of courses and study at various levels including Artisan Certificate, Craft Certificate, Diploma as well as other short courses.

Through education funding provided by the Ministry of Education and other funding agencies, children and youth can now access education through bursaries, government scholarships, and loans to cater for their tuition fees. Other education requirements are provided by well-wishers. We also do civic education in areas of environment protection and climate change, drug and substance abuse, Agriculture, social protection and Health promotion.
